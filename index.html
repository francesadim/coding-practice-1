My name is Frances Adimoraegbu, I was born and raised in Warri, Delta State in Nigeria. I was born to parents who had careers in the medical field. My dad is a trauma surgeon and my mum was a nurse. I left Nigeria at the age of 18 to study in the US. I attended school at Truman State University in Kirksville, Missouri. I was there for three semesters and then transferred to Minnesota State University Mankato, in Minnesota, where I met my husband Beyan Gonowolo. I graduated with a degree in Civil Engineering in 2014, and I currently work for the Minnesota Department of Health as a public health Engineer, which is a fancy way of saying I work in Health Care construction compliance.
